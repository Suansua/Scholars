## General Required Documents

### Identity Documents
- [ ] **Valid Passport**
  - Bio page copy (PDF)
  - Validity through at least December 2030

### Academic Transcripts
- [ ] **ZIMSEC O-Level Results**
  - Official certified copy
  - All subjects and grades
  
- [ ] **Cambridge A-Level Results/Predicted Grades**
  - Official document from school
  - If awaiting results: predicted grades letter
  - If results available: certified copy

### Proof of Financial Need
Mastercard Foundation specifically targets students who face financial barriers.
- [ ] **Financial Statement** (may be required)
  - Bank statements
  - Parent/guardian income documentation
  - Letter explaining financial circumstances

### Recommendations
- [ ] **Academic Recommendation 1**
  - From A-level teacher in relevant subject (Math/Physics)
  - Request at least 6 weeks before deadline
  
- [ ] **Academic Recommendation 2** (if required)
  - From school counselor or another teacher
  
- [ ] **Character/Community Recommendation** (if required)
  - From community leader, employer, or mentor

### Personal Statement/Essays
- [ ] **Personal Statement**
  - Focus on: background, challenges overcome, leadership, goals
  - Emphasize: commitment to Africa/giving back
  - Word limit: varies by partner (typically 500-1000 words)

- [ ] **Why This University/Program Essay** (if required)
  - Specific to chosen partner university

### English Proficiency (Verify Requirements)
- [ ] **Option A: IELTS/TOEFL scores** (if required)
- [ ] **Option B: Medium of Instruction Letter** (if accepted)
  - From school confirming English instruction
  - Must include: school letterhead, dates, signature

## Mastercard Foundation Program Values

Emphasize these in your application:
1. **Leadership potential** - examples of leading in school/community
2. **Commitment to Africa** - plans to contribute to Zimbabwe/continent
3. **Academic excellence** - strong A-level performance/predictions
4. **Overcoming adversity** - challenges faced and how you've persevered
5. **Community involvement** - service, volunteering, clubs
