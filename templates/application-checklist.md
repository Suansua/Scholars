## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Portal not working:**
- Try different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
- Clear cache and cookies
- Contact IT support: [Email]

**Document upload failing:**
- Check file format (PDF preferred)
- Reduce file size (compress PDF)
- Check filename (no special characters)

**Recommendation not received:**
- Follow up with recommender
- Check spam folder (if email-based)
- Contact scholarship office

**Missing confirmation:**
- Check spam/junk folder
- Log into portal to verify submission
- Contact scholarship office
